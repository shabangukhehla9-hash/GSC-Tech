Our Mission

At GSC Tech Support, we are committed to providing exceptional technical assistance that empowers our organization to operate efficiently and effectively. Our mission is to deliver rapid, reliable, and professional IT support services that minimize downtime and maximize productivity across all departments.

Who We Are

Your dedicated IT support team

GSC Tech Support is the dedicated IT help desk team for Global Sales Consultancy. We serve over 44 workstations across Marketing, HR, and Sales departments, providing comprehensive technical support for hardware, software, network connectivity, and system maintenance.

Our team consists of experienced IT professionals who are passionate about technology and committed to delivering outstanding customer service. We understand that technology issues can disrupt your workflow, which is why we prioritize quick response times and effective solutions.

What We Do

Comprehensive IT support services

  • Hardware Support: Troubleshooting and maintenance for desktops, laptops, printers, and peripherals
  • Software Assistance: Installation, configuration, and troubleshooting of business applications
  • Network Support: Connectivity issues, VPN access, and network resource management
  • Account Management: Password resets, access permissions, and user account administration
  • Asset Management: Tracking and maintaining computer inventory and replacement schedules
  • Training & Documentation: User guidance and comprehensive support documentation
