How to Service Ticket Requests

1

Review Ticket Details

Read the incident description, check priority level, and review any previous notes or history.

2

Contact the User

Reach out via phone or email to gather additional information and confirm the issue details.

3

Follow Troubleshooting Guide

Use the systematic troubleshooting questions to identify the root cause.

4

Apply Solution

Implement the fix, test thoroughly, and confirm with the user that the issue is resolved.

5

Document & Close

Record the solution, update ticket status, and log resolution time for future reference.

!

Escalate if Needed

If unable to resolve, follow the escalation policy and transfer to appropriate level.
