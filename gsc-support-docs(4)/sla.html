7. Exclusions

This SLA does not cover:

  • Issues caused by unauthorized modifications or third-party software
  • Problems resulting from user error or negligence
  • Hardware failures due to physical damage or misuse
  • Scheduled maintenance windows (with advance notice)
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, power outages, etc.)
  • Issues with personal devices not managed by IT

8. User Responsibilities

To ensure efficient support delivery, users are expected to:

  • Provide accurate and detailed information when reporting issues
  • Respond promptly to requests for additional information
  • Follow IT policies and security guidelines
  • Attempt basic troubleshooting before contacting support
  • Report issues in a timely manner
  • Maintain regular backups of critical data

9. Service Review

This SLA is reviewed quarterly and updated as needed to reflect changes in service delivery, technology, or business requirements. Feedback from users is actively solicited and incorporated into service improvements.
